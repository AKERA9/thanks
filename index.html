From Ideas to Impact.

Yattin epitomizes the new-age entrepreneur: energetic, solutions-driven, and forward-thinking, with a unique ability to identify industry gaps and convert them into powerful products, he is committed to delivering utmost safety in every wire his company produces. Beyond business, Yattin is a dance enthusiast and a strong believer in living life with energy and creativity—values that reflect in his work and leadership.


Hustle All Day, Everyday.

At just 29, Yattin Anand has carved a unique place as a dynamic millennial entrepreneur, known for his relentless drive, innovative thinking, and belief in turning challenges into opportunities. A graduate in commerce from Delhi University, Yatin authored the world’s first book on fan regulators, earning early recognition as a thought leader in the electrical industry.

With an eye on global standards, Yatin founded FCL Wire & Cable, introducing India’s first short-circuit proof wire capable of withstanding temperatures up to 300°C — a product that is also patent-protected, highlighting his commitment to original, problem-solving innovation.
